FAQ
How do I accept a ride?

When a ride appears, tap Accept within 10 seconds. If you miss it, it goes to the next driver.

How do I update my location?

Your location updates automatically when you're online. Make sure location permissions are granted.

What if I have an emergency?

Hold the red SOS button for 3 seconds. Dispatch will be alerted immediately with your GPS location.
