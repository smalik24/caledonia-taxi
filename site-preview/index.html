About Us

Hamilton's hometown taxi company

Caledonia Taxi was built by a local family who believed that getting around Hamilton shouldn't be complicated, expensive, or unreliable. We started with one car and one mission: be the taxi company we'd want to call.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of vehicles and use cutting-edge dispatch technology to get you where you need to go — quickly, safely, and affordably.

Punctuality We value your time. Our average pickup is under 10 minutes.
Safety All drivers are licensed, insured, and background-checked.
Transparency Upfront fare estimates. No surge pricing surprises.
