Admin live tracking — what do you want to see?

When you open the admin panel, how should the driver map look?

A

Full-screen map with overlay panels

The map fills the entire admin screen. Driver pins are live on the map. Clicking a pin opens a side panel showing that driver's current booking, status, and contact info. Active bookings appear as route lines on the map. Like Google Maps with Fleet Tracking.

Dedicated Tracking tab in admin

Tracking is its own full-width tab in the admin panel. The tab shows a large map with all driver pins, a legend (available/busy/offline), and a collapsible driver list on the side. Other tabs (Orders, Revenue, etc.) stay separate and uncluttered.
